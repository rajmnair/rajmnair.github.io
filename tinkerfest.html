Tinkerfest

Two Day program covering Product designing specially designed for Niti Aayog Initiative Atal Tinkering Labs under Atal Innovation Mission

Tinkerfest cover a rapid round of product designing training with module of learnin basic skills of Arduino Electronics , 3D printing and Prototyping skills

At last they have to pitch thier product with a presenataion and a small movie. with a overview of what they made and what they learn.
